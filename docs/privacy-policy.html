Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Hedaya ("the app") is an Islamic worship companion app for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Hedaya does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains entirely on your device.

Location

The app requests access to your device location only while the app is in use, solely to calculate accurate daily prayer times for your area. Your location is never stored permanently, never shared with third parties, and never used for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following is saved locally using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults) on your device only:

  • Prayer tracking progress and streak
  • Quran reading progress
  • Dhikr (Sebha) counter
  • App appearance preference (light/dark/auto)
  • Worship plan and onboarding answers
  • Prayer calculation method preference

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Hedaya does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking frameworks. No data is shared with any third party.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications (e.g., prayer time reminders, Sebha goal alerts). These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Hedaya does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on github.com/ahmedatya/Hedaya.
